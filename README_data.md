# Generating a Spatially Explicit Synthetic Population of Individuals and Households
## Data

#### *Utrecht University, The Netherlands. 2022 - 2023*
#### *Author: Marco Pellegrino*
#### *Contribution: Jan de Mooij, Tabea Sonnenschein, Mehdi Dastani, Dick Ettema, Brian Logan, Judith A. Verstegen*

All the data sets for this case study are openly available from the Dutch statistics institute CBS.

### Individual-based data sets:

-   **Marginal distribution by neighbourhoods.**
    -   Aggregation level: neighbourhoods
    -   Year: 2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://opendata.cbs.nl/statline/portal.html?_la=nl&_catalog=CBS&tableId=84583NED&_theme=236)
-   **Gender and age**.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year: 2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://opendata.cbs.nl/statline/portal.html?_la=nl&_catalog=CBS&tableId=03759ned&_theme=267)
-   **Migration background**, gender and age groups.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year: 2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://opendata.cbs.nl/statline/portal.html?_la=nl&_catalog=CBS&tableId=84910NED&_theme=267)
-   **Current education**, migration background, gender, and age groups.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year: 2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://opendata.cbs.nl/statline/portal.html?_la=nl&_catalog=CBS&tableId=71450ned&_theme=341)
-   **Car and moped license ownership,** and age groups.
    -   Aggregation level: South Holland province
    -   Year:  2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/83488NED)
-   **Household position**, gender and age groups.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year: 2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/71488NED)

### Household-based data sets:

-   **Standardized income group,** and household type.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year:  2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/85064NED)
-   **Car ownership,** and household type.
    -   Aggregation level: national
    -   Year: 2015
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/81845NED)

### Household-grouping data sets:

-   **Children in households**.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year:  2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/71486NED)
-   **Gender in couples**.
    -   Aggregation level: national
    -   Year:  2020
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/37772eng)
-   **Age disparity in couples**.
    -   Aggregation level: national
    -   Year:  2017

-   **Age disparity between mother and older child**.
    -   Aggregation level: municipality
    -   Year:  2019
    -   Online source: [CBS](https://www.cbs.nl/nl-nl/cijfers/detail/37201)
